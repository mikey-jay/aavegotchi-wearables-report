Wearable Cores Supply¶

Cores determine the slot and rarity of a forged wearable. The Core Emissions Framework, which was adopted by the Aavegotchi DAO in AGIP 57, sets standard for the distribution of cores. The document also includes targets for the distribution of slot cores within each rarity. The core framework targets are listed in the charts below for reference.
